Compliance as a data engineering problem

Validate your infrastructure against the standards that actually apply.

Privateer is a validation framework that runs automated checks against your resources with plugins, and then writes structured, machine-readable evidence in YAML, JSON, or SARIF. Built to operate at the Evaluation layer of the Gemara GRC engineering model.
